My first time out of the country to see the world...
...was a trip to Rome to view the work of Michelangelo. I literally spend a week visiting the Pieta. Mesmerized. May 21, 1972 a mentally disturbed geologist, walked into the chapel and attacked the sculpture with a hammer, breaking off several pieces. I cried as the tour guide recounted that story. People clamored for the fragments, understandably. They got most of the pieces back, aside from the nose.
